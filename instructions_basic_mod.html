About this HIT:

  • Please only participate in this HIT if you have normal color vision.

  • It should take about 15 minutes.

  • You will take part in an experiment involving visual perception. You will shown 2 images. Your task it to determine if the images are the exact same or different. Even small differences count as different.
  • You will complete a short practice (less than 1 minute) before starting the main task.
